Who We Are

We are Trailblazers, we are a solid group of software developers from bowen universities. We develop quality mobile and web applications.

All our applications are built using the Rapid Application Development (RAD) software development lifecycle. Building projects as a Trailblazer involves 4 main procedures. They are;

Define

Usually our first step is to do proper project definition. In this stage all our user stories are outlined properly. App functionalities are explicitely defined also. All team members are given roles based on the project on ground and application development progresses are solidly tracked.

Design

In this stage our UI/UX designers provide quality wireframes/prototypes for our front end developers to work on. All aspects of our application interface (both mobile applications and web applications) are handled properly at this stage.

Build

As a Trailblazer, this is the stage we all look up to. This is where all our application's business logic is built. our serverside (backend) developers work hand in hand with our Frontend developers(both mobile and web) to bring both the design and app functionalities to life.

Launch

Testing and deployment is handled at this stage. our Quality Assurance personnels perform unit and other forms of testing on the already developed application. any bug found at this stage is immediately corrected by the developers and the application is finally deployed.
